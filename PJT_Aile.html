✈️ Glider Wing Design – Engineering Project

Aerodynamic and structural design of a high-performance glider wing using advanced CAD and FEA tools.

The Project

This project focused on the aerodynamic and structural design of a glider wing. The process included airfoil selection, 3D CAD modeling, mesh generation, and finite element analysis (FEA) using Siemens CCM. The goal was to optimize the wing for maximum lift-to-drag ratio while ensuring mechanical integrity under flight loads. Teamwork, simulation, and engineering analysis were key skills developed throughout the project.

Simulation & Optimization

Finite element analysis was conducted to evaluate stress distribution and deformation under aerodynamic loads. The results guided the optimization of the wing geometry for both performance and safety. The final design achieved a balance between lightweight construction and structural robustness.
